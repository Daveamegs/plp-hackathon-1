About Me

Hello, I'm David — a passionate and self-driven full stack developer with a strong foundation in Python and Flask. My tech journey began on the backend, where I honed my skills building RESTful APIs and server-side applications. As I gained more experience, my curiosity led me beyond writing code — I became deeply interested in how applications are deployed, maintained, and scaled in real-world environments.

That curiosity opened the door to DevOps. I dove into containerization with Docker, explored orchestration using Kubernetes, and worked with CI/CD pipelines to automate deployments. Through hands-on projects and continuous learning, I built a solid understanding of modern software delivery practices — bridging the gap between development and operations.

Alongside that journey, I had some basic exposure to the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), and I was fascinated by how powerful and dynamic full stack JavaScript development could be. To deepen my frontend and JavaScript skills, I joined a full stack MERN bootcamp — an opportunity to upgrade my knowledge and become more versatile as a developer.

My goal is to build user-focused, scalable applications that solve real-world problems. Whether it's backend systems, frontend interfaces, or deployment pipelines — I enjoy understanding every layer of the stack. I'm always learning, always building, and always ready for the next challenge.
